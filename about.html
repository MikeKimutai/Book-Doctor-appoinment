ABOUT US

If you are registered with a GP surgery, you can use their online services to book, check or cancel appointments with a healthcare professional. If you have already signed up to online services, log in to your usual service provider and book one of the available appointments. To change an appointment online, you will need to cancel the appointment you already have and book a new one.
